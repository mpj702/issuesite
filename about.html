Not a new safety issue

Rail safety is not a new issue. The issue has been around since trains were invented. While it isn’t a new issue, it is still important to talk about. We have been observing railroading in the 21st century. As railfans and volunteer railroaders, we would like to teach the importance of rail safety.

There are different ways to watch trains such as virtual railfan. With VR you can watch a live stream from the comfort of your own home. With that you can also watch people make bad decisions. It doesn’t stop there either. Everyone has a cell phone, videos of trains hitting cars, trucks, and people are all over the internet. It hurts us to see or hear that another person has been injured or killed because they made a bad decision.
